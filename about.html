About Me

I am Janit Singh, an aspiring video editor, web designer, and content creator from Guru Nanak Khalsa College, Karnal, currently pursuing an MSc in Computer Science. I am a graduate of BSc Computer Science from Chhotu Ram Arya College, Sonepat.

I run the YouTube channels “Janit Edit”, where I create cinematic and entertainment edits, and “Janit Gaming” for gaming content. You can also find my work on Instagram @janit_edit_. Skilled in C, Java, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, I specialize in video editing, overlays, green screen clips, and creating epic cinematic edits.

I am passionate about creative storytelling and exploring technologies like blockchain, ChatGPT, and robotics. Dedicated to personal growth, I hit the gym twice daily and aim to inspire others through my creative projects and digital expertise.
